# Hermes Workspace — AI Companion for the BD & Sales Team

> *An intelligent workspace that gives every sales rep the knowledge and analytical power of a senior consultant, cloud architect, and deal strategist — all in one place.*

---

## The Problem

Our BD and sales teams face three challenges every day:

1. **Information overload** — Product updates, pricing changes, competitor moves, and customer intelligence are scattered across emails, Slack, shared drives, and individual heads.
2. **Inconsistent preparation** — The quality of customer meetings, proposals, and competitive responses depends heavily on individual experience and memory.
3. **Time spent searching, not selling** — Reps spend hours hunting for the right slide, the right number, or the right talking point instead of engaging customers.

The result: deals move slower, opportunities are missed, and our best people spend their energy on research instead of relationships.

---

## What Is Hermes Workspace?

Hermes Workspace is an AI-powered companion built specifically for our cloud sales and business development team. It gives every team member instant access to:

### Institutional Knowledge
Upload any document — competitor decks, customer briefs, pricing sheets, meeting notes, RFPs — and the system remembers everything. When you need information, it finds it instantly.

### Consulting-Grade Analysis
Describe a customer situation and get a structured analysis: stakeholder mapping, competitive positioning, value quantification, and a recommended approach. Not generic advice — specific, actionable guidance.

### Real-Time Deal Support
Preparing for a C-level meeting? Building a business case? Responding to a competitive threat? Ask the companion and get immediate, structured output you can use directly in your customer conversations.

## How It Works (Simple)

1. **Upload** — Drag and drop any document: PDF, PowerPoint, Word, Excel, or text files. Customer RFPs, competitor decks, internal pricing, meeting notes — it all goes in.

2. **Ask** — Talk to the companion in plain language. "How do we position against AWS for this manufacturing client?" or "Build me a stakeholder map for this deal."

3. **Get** — Receive structured, actionable output you can use immediately in your next customer interaction.

The system learns from everything your team uploads. Every document shared makes the entire team smarter.
